Christopher Columbus (Italian: Cristoforo Colombo 
was an Italian explorer, navigator, and coloniaer. Born in the Republic of Genoa,[3] under the auspices 
of the Catholic Monarchs of Spain he completed four voyages across the Atlantic Ocean. Those voyages and 
his efforts to establish settlements on the island of Hispaniola initiated the permanent European coloniaation 
of the New World.

colonies, motivated by imperialism and economic competition, Columbus proposed to reach
